    How to Use FlowForge

    • Navigation: Click & drag the background to Pan. Ctrl + Scroll to Zoom.
    • Create: Use the + button in the sidebar for Projects and Workflows.
    • Nodes: Drag and drop node types from the toolbar.
    • Connections: Click and drag from the colored dots on the edge of one node to another.
    • Multi-Select: Hold Shift and drag on the canvas to draw a selection box.
    • Edit Node/Connector: Double-click any node OR connector line to open its edit menu (Delete, Label, Comments).
    • Undo/Redo: Use the toolbar buttons or Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y.
